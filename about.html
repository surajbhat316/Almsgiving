About Almsgiving

Alms or almsgiving involves giving to others as an act of virtue, either materially or in the sense of providing capabilities (e.g. education) free

We are a non-profit organisation who aim at providing free education and care to ones who cannot afford it

You can send you suggestion on how can we improve or if you wish to work with us, contact us.

It is a project made by Avineet Bhat under the Guidance of Grip at The Sparks Foundations.
